Email Notification Flow

Flow: Form Dibuat ➔ Email to Step 1 ➔ Approved ➔ Email to Step 2 ➔ ... ➔ Final Approval ➔ Email to Creator
  • Signed URL: Link approval di email berlaku selama 7 hari.
  • Action: Approver dapat melakukan Approve atau Reject (dengan alasan).
  • Rejection: Jika ditolak di salah satu step, status menjadi Rejected dan proses berhenti.
